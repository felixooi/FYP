Threshold Selection Rationale
===========================
Business objective prioritizes catching at-risk employees (high recall),
while keeping operational load manageable.
Constraint sweep used: [0.5, 0.6, 0.7].
Selected constraint: Precision >= 0.70.
Interpretation: selected operating point balances false-positive workload for HR
and recall of at-risk employees using validation-first model selection.
For Precision >= 0.50, at least 1 in 2 flagged employees is expected to truly resign,
which bounds false-positive workload for HR while preserving high recall.
